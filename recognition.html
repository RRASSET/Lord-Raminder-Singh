Philanthropy

Champion Of Political &
Social Change

For Lord Rami Ranger, success is measured not only by personal achievements but by the positive impact one can make on society. Guided by his mother’s principles of empathy and selflessness, Lord Rami has dedicated his time, resources, and influence to uplifting communities and supporting causes that foster education, healthcare, and social unity. His philanthropic efforts reflect a deep commitment to giving back, improving lives, and building a more inclusive and compassionate world.

Queen’s Awards for Enterprise (2009 - 2013)

Sun Mark Ltd was honoured with the Queen’s Award for Enterprise in International Trade for five consecutive years, marking Lord Rami’s company as a leader in British exports.

Doctor of Letters (D.Litt) from the University of West London (2016)

This honorary degree celebrates Lord Rami’s significant contributions to business and his support for educational initiatives. His donation of £200,000 to enhance student facilities at the university reflects his belief in the power of education to shape future leaders.

Doctorate from Preston University (USA) (2016)

Awarded in recognition of Lord Rami’s exceptional contributions to international trade and his ongoing work in promoting global commerce and community service.

Freeman of the City of London

In recognition of his charitable work and support for British trade and industry, Lord Rami was granted the prestigious title of Freeman of the City of London.

Man of the Year — GG2 Leadership Awards (2014)

At the GG2 Leadership Awards, Lord Rami was honoured as Man of the Year by the Prime Minister, Rt Hon. David Cameron MP, for his leadership, community work, and contributions to strengthening ties between British and Asian communities.

European Business Awards: Export Champion of the Year (2012/2013)

Lord Rami was named Export Champion of the Year at the prestigious European Business Awards, recognising his outstanding achievements in international trade and his leadership in expanding British exports to over 130 countries through Sun Mark Ltd.

Hot 100 Investec Award (2013)

Lord Rami was named a recipient of the Hot 100 Investec Award, acknowledging his outstanding entrepreneurial success and business leadership across various sectors.

Patron of Combat Stress

Recognized for his generous donation and support of veterans’ mental health services through Combat Stress, a leading UK charity.

Support for Gandhi Memorial

Lord Rami contributed £100,000 to the Gandhi Memorial Foundation, helping to erect the Mahatma Gandhi statue in London’s Parliament Square. His efforts were celebrated for honouring Gandhi’s legacy of peace and non-violence.

“Winning The Queen’s Award Is The Pinnacle Of Business Success In Britain. To Have Done It Five Times In A Row Is Something I Could Only Have Dreamed Of.”
