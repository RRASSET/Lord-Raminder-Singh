CHAMPION OF POLITICAL & SOCIAL CHANGE

For Lord Rami Ranger, success is measured not only by personal achievements but by the positive impact one can make on society. Guided by his mother’s principles of empathy and selflessness, Lord Rami has dedicated his time, resources, and influence to uplifting communities and supporting causes that foster education, healthcare, and social unity. His philanthropic efforts reflect a deep commitment to giving back, improving lives, and building a more inclusive and compassionate world.

The Lord Rami Ranger FRSA Centre for Graduate Entrepreneurship: The Lord Rami Ranger FRSA Centre for Graduate Entrepreneurships at London South Bank University, made possible through a generous donation.

Supporting Student Facilities: His £250,000 donation to the University of West London funded the creation of a new pavilion for students, enhancing study and accommodation facilities.

The Pakistan, India & UK Friendship Forum: After the tragic 7/7 London bombings, Lord Rami founded this forum to unite British Indians and Pakistanis.
